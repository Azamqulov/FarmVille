Game Features

Discover what makes FarmVille 3 a delightful farming adventure

Adorable Animals

Raise and care for over 150 different breeds of animals, from chickens and cows to exotic creatures. Feed them, love them, and watch them grow from babies to adults!

Farm Customization

Design and build your dream farm with beautiful decorations, buildings, and crops. Express your creativity and create a unique farming paradise that reflects your personal style.

Social Farming

Join a farming co-op with friends or make new ones! Trade resources, help each other with farm tasks, and compete in friendly competitions to see who can build the most impressive farm.

Meet the Animals

Discover the adorable animals you can raise in FarmVille 3

Chickens

Raise chickens to produce eggs that can be used in various recipes or sold for coins.

Cows

Cows provide milk that can be turned into cheese, butter, and other dairy products.

Sheep

Sheep produce wool that can be crafted into fabric and clothing items.

Pigs

Pigs help find rare items and truffles when they explore your farm.

And many more animals to discover in the game!
